A Day of Discovery: Session Timeline

The training day is structured to build knowledge progressively, blending foundational theory in the morning with execution strategies in the afternoon.

Morning Session: Foundation & Planning (8:30 AM - 12:00 PM)

Part 1: Defining the Problem

Focuses on understanding the bigger picture with Systems Thinking and drilling down to root causes using Problem Tree Analysis.

Part 2: Planning with People in Mind

Introduces tools for identifying and engaging key individuals through Stakeholder Analysis and applying human-centered solutions with Design Thinking.

Mid-Day Session: The Project Blueprint (12:00 PM - 1:00 PM)

Part 3: Structuring for Success

Covers creating a project's strategic foundation, explaining the 'why' with a Theory of Change and the 'how' with a Logic Framework, all within the standard PM Cycle.

Afternoon Session: Execution & People (2:00 PM - 5:00 PM)

Part 4: Execution & Control

Dives into the mechanics of project execution, including scheduling, control, and the leader's role in coaching for performance.

Part 5: Managing the Human Element

Addresses the critical skill of guiding teams through transitions with effective Change Management strategies.

Core Competency Areas

The ten topics are grouped into three core areas, ensuring a balanced development of strategic, analytical, and leadership capabilities.

Emphasis on Application

The session is designed to be highly interactive, dedicating significant time to hands-on activities that translate theory into practical skills.

Empowering Leaders, Driving Results.
